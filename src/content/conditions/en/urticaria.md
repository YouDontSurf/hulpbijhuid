## What is Urticaria?

Urticaria is a allergic skin reaction that can cause significant concern when symptoms appear. Early recognition and proper management can make a big difference in outcomes. This article provides
 comprehensive information about symptoms, causes, and treatment options.

## Symptoms

- Visible changes in skin appearance or texture
- Itching, burning, or discomfort in the affected area
- Redness, swelling, or inflammation
- Possible spreading or changes over time

## Causes & Risk Factors

- Environmental factors and exposures
- Genetic predisposition in some cases
- Immune system responses
- Lifestyle and hygiene factors

## Treatment Options

### Self-Care
- Maintain good skincare routines
- Avoid known triggers and irritants
- Keep the affected area clean and protected

### Medical Treatments
- Topical medications prescribed by a doctor
- Oral medications for more severe cases
- Professional procedures when indicated
- Regular follow-up with a healthcare provider

## When to See a Doctor

Consult a healthcare provider if symptoms persist, worsen, significantly impact daily life, or if you notice signs of infection such as increased redness, swelling, warmth, or pus. Early medical evaluation can prevent complications and improve outcomes.
